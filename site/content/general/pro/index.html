Frequently asked questions

How the "Lifetime Usage" works?

After purchase, you can use TWE in your projects forever. You will get updates & support for the first 12 months. After that, you can renew the license to get another year of updates & support. If you don't renew, you can keep using TWE in the lastest version released during the 12-month period.

Can I test TWE before purchase?

Certainly! We recommend you try out TWE, you can download it for free here. The free version includes all components. The main difference is the free version is licensed under AGPL. In order to use TWE in your commercial projects without AGPL limitations, you need to purchase a commercial license.

Can I use TWE for commercial purposes?

Yes, after you purchase a commercial license, you can use TWE in your commercial projects without AGPL limitations. You can create and sell website or app projects, you can use TWE in your SaaS business, you can create templates or themes for sale, build a CRM and ERP tools etc.

Do you accept returns / refunds?

Yes, if you are unhappy with TWE for any reason, you can request a refund within the first 60 days of purchase. We highly encourage you to try out TWE before purchase, you can download it for free here.

How many projects can I build with TW Elements

The number of commercial projects that you can build with the license is specified in the license tier. You can choose between 1 project in the BASIC tier, 5 projects in EXTENDED or unlimited projects in UNLIMITED.

What does the "project" mean in the license?

The project is defined as a domain / app. Even with 1 project you can have multiple subdomains i.e. you can publish your app on example.com domain, and have an additional module at module.example.com. With the UNLIMITED license, you can publish your projects on as many domains as you want.

How many developers can use TWE license?

TWE licenses are sold individually, for a single developer. If you need a team license, with multiple developers, contact us at tailwind@mdbootstrap.com.

Will I get an invoice? Is VAT included in the price?

You will get an invoice, and the VAT is already included in the price. During the checkout process, you will be able to fill out your company details (including VAT ID) and get an invoice based on them. If you forgot yo fill out your company details, you can do so after the purchase. Just contact us at tailwind@mdbootstrap.com.

Can I sell templates, themes or UI Kits that I build with TWE?

No, in accordance with the commercial license you can't sell templates or UI Kits that are direct competition to TWE. You can build an app or website template for sale, build a system like admin dashboards, CRMs, ERPs, a front-end for a subscription service etc. but you can't sell a template that is a direct copy of TWE.
